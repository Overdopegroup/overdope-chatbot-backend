🎬 VIDEO PRODUCTION (MUSIC VIDEO / COMMERCIAL / REELS)

Q: How much does a music video, commercial, advertising video, or reel cost?
A: Costs vary depending on the type and complexity of the project. Pricing is affected at three key stages:
- Pre-Production – concept/script, mood boards, locations, casting, set design
- Production – camera(s), filming duration, lighting, choreography, crew, styling
- Post-Production – editing, VFX, grading, sound design
We offer fixed packages and fully custom quotes depending on your needs.

Q: What’s included in the basic music video shoot? Do you offer fixed packages?
A: Yes, we offer both fixed-price and custom packages.
- Cyclorama Basic Music Video (€250): 30–40min shoot, our studio, full color background, basic edit (same day), 1 promo photo. Ideal for freestyles/social promos.
- Outdoor Basic Music Package: €600 Nicosia | €680 Larnaca | €720 Limassol/Famagusta | €760 Paphos. Up to 4hr filming, 1 camera, 1 edit up to 2.5min, teaser, 1 photo, delivery 5–10 days.

Q: Do you provide locations or do I need to find one?
A: We offer location scouting (€150–€600) or you can use our studios. All locations must be pre-approved.

Q: What equipment do you use?
A: In-house: Blackmagic Cinema 6K, 2x Lumix S1H, studio lighting, Mavic Pro 2 drone, smoke machine, gimbal, modifiers. We can also rent special gear if needed.

Q: Is lighting included in the shoot?
A: Yes for all cyclorama shoots. Outdoor shoots only if requested.

Q: Can I shoot in your studio or on location?
A: You can choose—studio, outdoor, or custom location.

Q: Do you offer editing?
A: Yes—cutdowns, transitions, grading, lyrics on screen, sound sync. No 3D animation or advanced motion graphics currently.

Q: How many revisions are included?
A: Each video includes 1 free revision. More can be added for a fee.

Q: Can I bring my own videographer/editor?
A: Yes, if you inform us in advance. We’re not responsible for final results from external teams.

Q: What if I want to rent the studio only?
A: Cyclorama Rental includes lights and basic gear. 2hr–€110, 3hr–€160, 4hr–€205, 5hr–€235, 6hr–€275, 7hr–€315, 8hr–€355. Monthly 25hr–€1000. Add €20 if you need us to handle files.

🎙️ PODCAST & TALKING HEAD PRODUCTIONS
Q: How much does it cost to shoot a podcast at Overdope?

A:
At Overdope, you have two main options for shooting your podcast, depending on your budget and production goals:

1. Affordable, Ready-Fast Option – ATEM Mini Pro (Live Switch):

Your podcast is recorded live using our multi-cam ATEM Mini Pro switcher.
You get a finished, ready-to-post Full HD video (no separate camera feeds).
Pros: Fastest turnaround (same/next day), budget-friendly, great for interviews and quick content.
Cons: Lower resolution (Full HD, not 4K/6K), camera angles are “baked in” (can’t change them later).
2. High-End, Flexible Editing – RAW Multicam (6K/4K):

Each camera records in high resolution (6K/4K) straight to SD cards.
All files are synced and edited in post-production, giving you maximum control over cuts, angles, graphics, and export format.
Pros: Best possible quality, flexible edits, advanced post-production options.
Cons: Higher cost, longer delivery, larger files.
📦 Podcast Studio Rental Options

Studio Rental (bring your own camera):
€50/hr, €90 (2hr), up to €310 (8hr). Includes pro mics, lights, and audio recorder for up to 3 people.
With Our Cameras (no editing):
€120/hr, €230 (2hr), up to €750 (8hr). Add a 3rd camera for €30–€50/hr.
Real-Time Edit with ATEM Mini Pro:
€90/hr, €170 (2hr), up to €600 (8hr). Add 3rd camera: €15–€30/hr.
Editing:
ATEM footage: €150 (2 cams), €180 (3 cams).
Full 6K/4K edit: €250 (2 cams), €270 (3 cams).
Teasers/reels: €30–€80+.
Which option is best for you?

If you want a fast, affordable podcast to post right away: Go with ATEM Mini Pro (Live Switch)
If you want maximum quality, flexibility, and advanced editing: Choose RAW Multicam (6K/4K)
You can also choose to shoot with our cameras and crew, or just rent the studio and bring your own equipment and team.
